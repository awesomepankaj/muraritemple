Welcome to Murari Temple.

Murari Devi Temple is a beautiful & great place to visit in Sunder Nagar tehsil of Distt. Mandi. This Temple is in the west of Sunder Nagar on the top of a sacred hill named Murari Dhar. It is believed that this temple was made by Pandwas during their "AGYATWAAS". There are also some rocks on which some large human footprints can be seen and local people say that these foot prints are of Pandwas.
